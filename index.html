Indomitus Crusade: Tower of Abyss

Prologue — You are part of a vast Imperial host waging the Indomitus Crusade on the world of Sigra. The forces of Chaos have raised a black spire, the Tower of Abyss. You are chosen to enter, strike at its heart, and return to Angel's Watch—an Imperial stronghold—to resupply. Descend to Floor 10, defeat the guardian, and collapse the tower to free Sigra for the Imperium.

How to play

Pick a class and chapter/order/regiment, then push room by room. Encounters are combat, tests, or brief rests. Each combat round has a small chance for a random twist (1 in 6). Medkits are rare but fully heal. Armor reduces damage and raises AC. Kill foes to earn XP and Thrones (₮), scaled by floor. Return to Angel's Watch to shop. Level cap is 40; every 5 levels, gain +1 to a random stat.

Dice

Attacks: d20 + POW + 2 vs DEF; crit on 20. Damage scales with Power. Will boosts Pray healing. Armor reduces incoming damage and increases AC.
